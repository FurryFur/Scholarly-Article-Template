Model

Our model builds heavily on the WaveGAN architecture by [Author names here][Reference here]. The original WaveGAN implementation ported the DCGAN [Reference to original DCGAN paper] architecture to 1D to work with audio data. We replace the existing 1D DCGAN model with our own 1D implementation of Progressive GAN [paper reference]. We also extend the model to support conditioning text, taking heavy inspiration from StackGAN's [paper reference] text conditioning implementation.
